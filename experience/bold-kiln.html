TripShire is an experiential trips company that enables travelers to discover experiential trips and destinations. TripShire lets you explore/customize itineraries that can only be put together by an insider or a travel consultant and share/read trips experience at the comfort of your laptop or phone.

I worked with them as a backend developer and helped them built API for tripshire website and admin panel and also created Reactjs components for admin panel.

About The Product

If a person wants to go on a holiday trip then he/she has a lot of questions like shall I buy a holiday package online or from the local operator? What things and places should I explore on the trip and under budget? I want to alter my itineraries etc. To tackle this problem Tripshire plans to create a platform in which the user can explore and book itineraries that can only be put together by an insider or a travel consultant, share their travel experience by writing on the platform, users can also customize their itineraries.

My Role

I joined as a backend developer. My responsibility was to design the nodejs API for the blog, booking, payment, trips and authentication functionality. I also created Reactjs components for the admin panel through which admin can add and update trips.
